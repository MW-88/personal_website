Detecting aquatic plants populations for monitoring satellite images of the Nieuwkoopse Plassen

Aquatic plants play an important role in biodiversity within the Natura 2000 area of the Nieuwkoopse Plassen in the Netherlands. Using a pixel based random forest machine learning model,satellite images and a large amount of annotations we could annotate aquatic plants to monitor their total population annually with high accuracy. Unfortunately, the population of aquatic plants is decreasing each year. Measures need to be taken to reverse this trend and increase their population.
