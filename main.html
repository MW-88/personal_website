Dectecting aquatic plants in the Nieuwkoopse Plassen

Aquatic plants play an important role in biodiversity within the Natura 2000 area of the Nieuwkoopse Plassen in the Netherlands. Using a machine learning model and satellite images, we annotate aquatic plants to monitor their total population annually. Unfortunately, the population of aquatic plants is decreasing each year. Measures need to be taken to reverse this trend and increase their population.

This visualization highlights the growth patterns and distribution of aquatic plants observed in June 2023. The analysis identifies key factors influencing their habitat.

In September 2023, new patterns emerged, showing the seasonal impacts on aquatic plant ecosystems. This article explores these changes in detail.
